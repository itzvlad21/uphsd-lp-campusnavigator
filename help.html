Help and Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I navigate the campus map?

You can use the touch controls to explore the map or use the search bar to find specific locations.

What should I do if the app is not working?

Please ensure you have a stable internet connection and try refreshing the page. If the issue persists, contact support.

Can I use the app as a guest?

Yes, guests can log in and use the navigation features without creating an account.
