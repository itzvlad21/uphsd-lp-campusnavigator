About the Virtual Campus Navigator

The Virtual Campus Navigator is an innovative 3D tool designed to help students, faculty, and visitors explore the University of Perpetual Help System DALTA Las Pinas campus with ease. This interactive platform provides a detailed virtual representation of the campus, allowing users to navigate through various facilities, locate important landmarks, and access essential information.

About the University

The University of Perpetual Help System DALTA, located in Las Pinas, Philippines, is committed to providing quality education and fostering a conducive learning environment. The campus is equipped with modern facilities, dedicated faculty, and a vibrant student community, making it an ideal place for academic and personal growth.
